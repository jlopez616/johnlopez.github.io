Software Development Projects

Ballona Discovery

For my senior project, I am collaborating with Anthony Boyac and Jialing Li to develop Ballona Discovery, a content management system for the Ballona Discovery Park. Park staff and volunteers will be able to use a web based interface to design and publish interpretive and interactive mobile web pages - without writing a single line of code! These pages will be visible through "tours" as guests walk through the park.

The project will be launched in early December, 2019. You may follow its progress on our GitHub.

StoneScript

StoneScript is an esoteric programming language themed - it is the oldest programming language ever discovered! Written in JavaScript using the Ohm library, StoneScript compiles "stone tablets" written using the "most primitive" syntax into JavaScript.

To learn more about StoneScript, please visit the StoneScript website.

Research Experience

Novice Debugging in Block-Based and Hybrid Environments

This was the research project I completed as a participant of the Intelligent Multimodal Human-Computer Interaction Research Experience for Undergraduates (REU) at the University of Florida. This project, in collaboration with the LearnDialogue Group, investigated the abilites and perceptions of novice computer science students when debugging programs using Block-Based and Hybrid environments.

We are presently awaiting results for a poster presentation at SIGSCE 2020. You may view our proposal here.

GRNsight

As a member of the GRNsight development team, I performed several debugging tasks to improve the robustness of the software, and I implemented an interactive Gene Pages feature.
